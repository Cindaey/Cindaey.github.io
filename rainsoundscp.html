RainyDay Soundscape

This piece is one of Cindy's earlier animation projeects that was created and used for the purpose of studying sound in imageries and recreating the sound so that it ties cohesively to the image. The image was also accompanied by small animations in order to further piece together the sound and the image of the video and to an extent, animation would count as a factor that can bring both the sound and image together.
