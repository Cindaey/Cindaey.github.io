BFA Exhibition Promotional Materials(2021)

This is a client project with the primary purpose promoting the BFA exhibition of Spring 2022 across the campus and the region. The main message was the information relating to the BFA show in order to get people informed of the event; Specifically where is it taking place, when is it taking place, and a brief way (visually through design or through description) that tells people what is in the show. The project was done on the behalf of SUNY Oswego's BFA class of 2022.
